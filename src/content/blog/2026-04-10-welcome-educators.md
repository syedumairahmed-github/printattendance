Welcome to AttendanceSheet Pro! We built this platform because we understand the pain of managing manual attendance sheets. 

### Why We Built This

Teachers spend countless hours formatting Excel files, fixing formulas, and tracking down blank sheets. With our tool, all you need to do is paste your student list and hit **Export**.

Here are some top features:

1. **Smart Calendar Integration**: Automatically skips weekends. Mark regional holidays in seconds.
2. **Local First**: Your student data never leaves your browser. Absolute privacy guaranteed.
3. **Multiple Profiles**: Manage all your batches in one single place.

Stay tuned for more updates, and happy teaching!
